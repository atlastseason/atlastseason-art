2D Animation

Animate my life

Animation is about making things alive!

For 8 years I've been study and do animation and I think it's the most amazing and alluring medium to ever existed. Using what's at my disposal, digital manipulation such as Toonboom Harmony , Blender , Adobe Suites , Procreate Dream , or even the physical one like pen, paper and a little light table to create animation!

So that's why I want to get into the nitty gritty of all things that are animate-able , and then share to those who are the same interested, through my own take, of what animation can be!

Storyboard

Visualize the thoughts

Storyboard's work is about translation.

You can do storyboard on, imo, just everywhere you can think of. Pieces of paper for thumbnails. Any drawing program such as Photoshop. Or maybe the more specific one like Toonboom Storyboard Pro to make storyboard as easy as possible.

The idea's that everything start from the mind's of people. It can came from any fantasy world, futuristic sci-fi fiction. But our work as a Storyboard Artist is to came up with a vision that will speak to the director and the audience on what should be the final product.

The most important thing about my job is to make sure that the work gets delivered on time with the message that get through clearly.
