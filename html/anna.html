My Storyboard Work in “My Daemon”

At last, My Daemon has been released through Netflix Streaming Service. I've been honored to had done storyboard work for this show. From the first draft of the scripts through the final scene of the show, we have been working hard to achieve what the show promised. A boy and his companion, going on an adventure through a somewhat bleak but hopeful world of a broken world.

This is the trailer of the show.
Warning: Spoilers ahead! If you haven't watched the show, I highly encourage you to watch it first before seeing this, so you can get to look at both sides of what the storyboard is and how the result came to be!
